Documentation

About

  • The Nucleotide Nequence Translator is a simple tool that converts a DNA or mRNA sequence into an amino acid sequence
  • The converter outputs the +1, +2 and +3 reading frames of the input sequence
  • For convenience, the structure and properties of the amino acids are accessible from the menu

Use

  • Select a DNA or mRNA sequence to input
  • Ensure that the sequence only contains A,C,G and T/U
  • Note: Sequence length must be at least 6 nucleotides
  • Click "Convert"
  • Change between the amino acid codes
  • Note: If the length of the input sequence is not divisible by three (the length of a codon), the overhanging sequence will be removed as part of the conversion

Limitations

  • Mutations cannot be detected
  • Only one sequence can be converted at a time
  • The output does not align the nucleotide sequence with the amino acid sequence
